East Lawrence Middle School Mentor Program

Supporting Professional Growth, Building Stronger Educators

Why Become a Mentor?

Join our team of leaders and make a lasting impact on new teachers. ELMS mentors model excellence, support new educators, and help create a culture of continuous improvement.

Mentor Selection Process

  • Minimum of 3 years of successful teaching experience
  • Positive evaluation history and classroom management skills
  • Peer recommendations and administrative approval

Evaluation Criteria

  • Regular mentor-mentee meetings
  • Documented support logs
  • Feedback from mentees and instructional leaders
  • Professional growth goals achieved

Life-Long Learning Opportunities

  • Monthly professional learning communities (PLCs)
  • Access to regional and state-level PD events
  • Mentor workshops and leadership development series

Fostering a Collegial Culture

Our mentoring program strengthens collaboration across grade levels and content areas, promoting a culture of trust, reflection, and support. Peer observations and open feedback loops ensure every teacher grows in a positive environment.

Benefits Package

  • Stipends for mentor teachers
  • Recognition at faculty events and in school newsletters
  • Priority registration for PD workshops
  • Leadership pathway opportunities
